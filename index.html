Biography

I am a fourth (final) year Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), starting from Oct. 2020. I feel lucky to be advised by Prof. Michael R. Lyu . I was a visiting Ph.D. student at UCLA, working with Prof. Nanyun Violet Peng . I also work closely with Dr. Zhaopeng Tu from Tencent AI Lab and Prof. Pinjia He from CUHK(SZ). I received my B.E. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), in 2017.

Research Interests

The reliability and safety of AI models and software, such as large language models, multi-modal models, code intelligence models, and medical intelligence models.
